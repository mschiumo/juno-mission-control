## üîë The 5-Step Playbook

### Step 1: Learn the Foundations
- Understanding what AI agents can and cannot do
- No-code/low-code platform basics
- Core automation concepts

### Step 2: Identify High-ROI Opportunities
- Finding processes that are:
  - Repetitive
  - Time-consuming
  - Error-prone
  - High volume
- Prioritizing use cases by business impact

### Step 3: Map Out the Entire Process
- Documenting current workflows
- Identifying inputs, outputs, and decision points
- Understanding edge cases and exceptions

### Step 4: Plan the Architecture
- **Workflow vs. Agent decision:**
  - **Workflow:** Predefined steps, deterministic
  - **Agent:** Can make decisions, more flexible
- Choosing the right tool for the job

### Step 5: Get to a POC & Implement Guardrails
- Building a proof-of-concept quickly
- Testing with real data
- Adding safety measures and error handling
- Iterating based on feedback
