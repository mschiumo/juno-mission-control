## IMPORTANT RULES

### Document Validity
- **FBI records:** Must be less than **6 months old** at submission
- **Other documents:** Should be less than **90 days old** at submission
- **Birth/death certificates:** No time restrictions

### Apostille & Translation
- All US public documents must be **apostilled** by the issuing state
- Apostilled documents must be **officially translated** to Slovak
- **Both the document AND the apostille** must be translated

### Power of Attorney
- **Sign at the embassy** (not earlier — must be witnessed there)
- Authorizes lawyer to represent you for both applications
