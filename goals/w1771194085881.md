## Research Summary

### Key Findings:

**Apify Pricing:**
- Free: $0 (includes $5 credits/month)
- Starter: $29/month + pay-as-you-go
- Scale: $199/month + pay-as-you-go
- Business: $999/month + pay-as-you-go
- Compute Units: $0.20-$0.30 per CU (1 GB RAM/hour)

**Twitter/X API Options:**
- Official X API: $200/month (Basic) to $5,000/month (Pro)
- TwitterAPI.io: $0.15 per 1,000 tweets (pay-as-you-go, 97% cheaper)
- Free tier severely limited: 100 reads/month

**Financial News APIs:**
- Finnhub: FREE (60 req/min) - best free tier
- Alpha Vantage: FREE (25 req/day) - good for prototyping
- Polygon.io: $29/month (unlimited calls)
- NewsAPI.ai: $90/month (5K tokens, includes NLP)

### Recommended Budget-Friendly Setup:
1. **Development**: Finnhub (free) + TwitterAPI.io (pay-as-you-go)
2. **Production**: Finnhub (commercial license) + TwitterAPI.io + Apify (if scraping needed)
